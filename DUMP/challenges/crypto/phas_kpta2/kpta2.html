Secret algorithm (KPTA 2).


Description:

This encryption method is based on using an algorithm that doesn't require a key. The strength of this cryptosystem resides with the secrecy of the algorithm, not with the key.

That is a bad practise because once you know the method you can decrypt any text you want.
Your aim is to analyse some texts with their corresponding cipher texts and determine the algorithm. Once you know the algorithm decrypt the final text.
